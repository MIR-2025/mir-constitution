# Changelog

All notable changes to the MIR Constitution will be documented in this file.

The format is based on [Keep a Changelog](https://keepachangelog.com/en/1.0.0/).

---

## [1.0.0] - 2025-12-21

### Added
- Initial publication of the MIR Constitution
- Core principles: Purpose, Neutrality, Privacy, Platform Autonomy
- Explicit boundaries defining what MIR will never become
- "What MIR Is — and Is Not" section
- "History, Not Judgment" principle
- "Growth With Care" principle

### Origin
This constitution was first conceived in 2025 as MIR development began, formalizing the answer to the question: *"What service is the internet missing the most?"*

The answer: A neutral, portable participation history layer that restores continuity without surveillance.

---

## Future Changes

Any amendments to this constitution will be:
1. Documented in this changelog with date and rationale
2. Committed to this repository with a descriptive message
3. Reflected on the canonical source at [myinternetreputation.org/constitution](https://myinternetreputation.org/constitution)

Major philosophical changes require public notice and explanation.
